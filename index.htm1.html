True Love

A Story Beyond Life...

Final Part – Mudivu Alla, Maatram

This is not just a love story. This is a journey of the soul. Love that started as attraction... Turned into something divine. He never wanted her for himself, He only wanted her happiness. He hid his pain, Protected her peace, And chose silence over selfishness. He wrote poems, But they never reached her. Yet, they lived inside him. For some, love is part of life... But for him, love is life itself. He believed in God, Detached from material desires, But held onto one sacred wish— To be united with his love. Did love fail? No. Love never fails. It only transforms. Some love stories unite... Some elevate. This love... Elevated. This is not the end. This is just a beginning.
