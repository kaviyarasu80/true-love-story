TRUE LOVE 💔

Nanba… en kathai oru sadharana kadhal illa… idhu unmai, idhu vali, idhu aanmigam…

Naan avalai mudhal murai paarkum pothu, aval enakku oru penn illai… aval oru theivam pola therinthal…

Naan avalai santhoshamaga vaazha paarkka aasai patten… aval sirippai kaappatha aasai patten…

Aana oru naal… situation enna avalidam irunthu pirithathu… appo naan puriyala… adhu end nu… aana pinnadi purinjadhu… adhu oru test…

En kadhal mogam illai… athu dheivam serntha unarvu…

Oru naal kanavil naan avala paathen… aval veedu… aval kural… “ulle vaa”… naan thidikittu ezhunthen… athu en kadhal unmai nu urudhi pannina neram…

Adutha naal… oru bus… aval padikkum palli peyar… naan parthen… aval ullae irunthal…

Andha nimisham… kumbida pona dheivam munnadi vandhathu pola…

Aana… idhu serntha kathai illa… idhu pirivu aarambam…

Naan avalai daily parkka ponaen… aval sirithaal en ulagam velicham…

Oru naal aval ennai paathu sirithaal… adhu innum en kannula irukku…

Aana adutha naal… aval ennai paathu maraindhaal…

Appo purinjadhu… avalukku naan discomfort…

Adhanala… naan avalai disturb panna niruthinen…

Aana en manasu… avalai vida villai… avalukku theriyama naan avalai rasithen…

Oru naal… photo edukka aasai vandhadhu… aana naan tharmathukku kattu pattavan… naan edukkala…

Pinbu naan avalai paarkum pothu… enakku santhosham varala…

Aval mugathil sirippu illai… sorvu mattum irundhadhu…

Adhai paarkum pothu… en manasu udainthadhu…

En kadhal poi nu ninaichen… aana pinnadi purinjadhu… athu poi illa…

Naan avalai marakka muyarchi panninen… mudinthadhu…

Aana naan maranthadhu… avalai illa… ennai dhaan…

Naan yaar nu thedi ponavan… indru ennaiye izhanthen…

Indha thavippai theerka… rendu per dhaan mudiyum…

Oruvar Kadavul… matroruvar Aval…

Naan avalai ninaithu pala kavithaigal ezhuthinen…

“Irulal ulagam maraigirathu… un koonthalal antha irul kooda olirgirathu…”

“Kannodu kangal pesum mozhigal… kavigal ezhuthaatha kavithai…”

“Un kannir thuligalai kaana maruthavan… un punnagai thenai paruga thavithavan…”

Aana… indha kavithaigal… avalai seravillai…

Silarukku kadhal oru part of life… aana enakku… kadhal dhaan life…

Naan Sivanai vanangum oruvan… ennaal etharkkum aasai padavillai…

Aana aval visayathil… naan ennaal ennaiye maranthuten…

En kadhal avalai sernthadhu… aana aval ennai seravillai…

Idhu mudivu illa… idhu oru paguthi mattum…

Life never fails… Kadhalum mudiyadhu…

— TRUE LOVE NEVER ENDS —
